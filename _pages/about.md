I'm Rishi Latchmepersad, an engineer from Trinidad & Tobago writing about embedded systems, TinyML, STM32, ESP32, and practical machine learning work.

This site started as a place to document what I was learning in public. Over time, it has grown into a record of projects, experiments, setbacks, and small wins across embedded software and applied AI.

You'll find posts here about:

- STM32 and ESP32 development
- sensors, drivers, and debugging
- TinyML and model deployment
- ML experiments and paper-writing updates
- books, career reflections, and learning notes

If you're new here, a good place to start is the [archive](/archive/) or the latest posts on the [homepage](/).
