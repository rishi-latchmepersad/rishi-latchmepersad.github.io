# Making Embedded Systems

I also made a big push to finish some of the books on my list. The first one was the book by Elicia White: Making Embedded Systems.

I would highly recommend this book for anyone who wants to work or already works in the embedded systems field. Reading the book felt like I was having a chat with a really experienced embedded engineer, about all sorts of areas and projects she had worked on in her life. It's not a really in-depth book on any one area, but that's what makes it so applicable to so many engineers.

Based on this foundation, I think that most engineers would have a much better foundation, and could use the further readings etc. to get more in-depth knowledge of whatever area they're interested in.
